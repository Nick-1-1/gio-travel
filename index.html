Georgia

Visit Georgian Regions

Georgia is a picturesque country situated at the crossroads of Europe and Asia. It is surrounded by Russia, Turkey, Armenia, and Azerbaijan, with the Black Sea to its west. Due to its strategic location on the Silk Road, Georgia has been invaded several times throughout history, including by the Mongols, Ottoman Empire, Persians, and the Soviet Union.

Today, it is one of the safest countries to travel to and is becoming an increasingly popular destination. Its diverse landscapes and rich culture make it a jewel of the South Caucasus. The bustling cities of Tbilisi and Kutaisi are key highlights for any traveler.
